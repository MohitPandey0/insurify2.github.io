Motor Insurance

With a wide range of policies available, including comprehensive coverage, third-party liability coverage, and collision coverage, we can provide you with the protection you need while on the road.

Travel Insurance

With a wide range of policies available, including coverage for medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost or stolen luggage, we can provide you with the protection you need while on your trip.

Accident & Health Insurance

With a wide range of policies available, including coverage for hospital stays, medical procedures, and disability, we can provide you with the protection you need in case of an unexpected event.

Marine Insurance

Marine insurance is a type of insurance that protects against losses or damages that may occur to ships, cargo, terminals, and any transport or cargo by which property is transferred, acquired, or held between the points of origin and final destination.

Engineering Insurance

The engineering insurance policy covers a wide range of risks, including damages to equipment and machinery, delays in construction, and liability claims. It also covers the cost of professional fees, such as legal fees, in the event of a claim

Property Insurance

Property insurance is a type of insurance that covers the loss or damage of a person's property. This can include the person's home, car, or personal belongings. Property insurance helps to protect individuals and families from financial losses that may occur due to unexpected events, such as fires, burglaries, or natural disasters.

Home Insurance

Home insurance is a type of insurance that provides financial protection for homeowners against a variety of risks, including damages to the home, theft, and personal liability. It is an essential coverage for anyone who owns a home, as it helps to safeguard their investment and provide peace of mind.

Term Insurance

Term insurance is a type of life insurance that provides protection for a specific period of time, typically between 10 and 30 years. It is an affordable option for those looking to protect their loved ones in the event of their death, and it can also be used to cover debts or other financial obligations.

Liability Insurance

Insurance that helps protect individuals and businesses from potential financial losses resulting from legal claims made against them. It covers damages that the policyholder may be legally responsible for, such as bodily injury or property damage to a third party

Fire and Burglary Insurance

The policy covers damages caused by fires, including structural damages, personal property damages, and any additional living expenses incurred while your home or business is being repaired. It also covers damages caused by burglaries, including theft of personal property and damages to the property.
